## 3. How These Features Help in Daily Life (Regular User Perspective)
### 1. Communication 

Smooth performance allows apps like messaging, video calls, and social media to run without lag.

Efficient processors help during WhatsApp calls, online meetings, and browsing.

Good battery life ensures the phone lasts the entire day without frequent charging.

Example:
During online classes or long conversations, you don’t need to worry about your phone slowing down or running out of battery.

### 2. Photography 

AI camera features improve everyday photos.

Pixel 8a advantages:

AI removes unwanted objects from photos.

Automatic color and lighting correction.

Strong night photography.

S23 FE advantages:

Good portrait shots.

Better video stabilization.

Real-life use:

Taking photos during trips, family functions, or college events.

Editing photos instantly without needing extra apps.

### 3. Productivity 

Smartphones help in studying and work tasks.

Fast processors allow multitasking between notes, browsers, and video lectures.

Large displays improve reading PDFs or watching lectures.

AI features help with voice typing, photo scanning of documents, and smart suggestions.

Example:
A student can attend a lecture on YouTube while taking notes in another app without lag.

### 4. Entertainment 

Smooth performance improves gaming, streaming, and music apps.

High-quality displays enhance Netflix, YouTube, and movie watching.

Long battery means you can watch movies during travel.
## 5. Prompt Patterns for Each Aspect
### 1. Comparative Analysis Prompts

Used to compare two technologies or products.

Examples:

“Compare battery life and camera features of two smartphones.”

“How do Android and iOS differ in terms of performance and security?”

“Compare Google Pixel phones with Samsung Galaxy phones.”

### 2. Experiential Perspective Prompts

These ask for explanations from a specific user's viewpoint.

Examples:

“As a college student, how would a smartphone with AI features help in studies?”

“From a traveler’s perspective, explain the importance of a good smartphone camera.”

“As a content creator, what benefits do AI cameras provide?”

### 3. Everyday Functioning Prompts

These explain how technology works in daily life.

Examples:

“Describe how AI cameras improve everyday photography on smartphones.”

“How do smartphone batteries and power management affect daily usage?”

“Explain how smartphones help students with productivity and online learning.”
